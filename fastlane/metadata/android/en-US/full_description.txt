Ensemble is an unofficial, community-built mobile client for Music Assistant, providing both local playback and remote control of your music library.

Features:
* Stream music directly to your phone with background playback
* Control any speaker or device connected to Music Assistant
* Multi-room grouping capabilities
* Queue management with drag-to-reorder
* Background playback with media notifications
* Fast ~300ms response for play/pause controls

Ensemble supports both local playback (streaming to your phone) and remote control of any Music Assistant player in your home.

Note: Requires a running Music Assistant server. This is a community project and is not affiliated with the official Music Assistant team.
